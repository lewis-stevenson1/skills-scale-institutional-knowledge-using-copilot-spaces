# OctoAcme — Role-Specific Onboarding Checklists

## Purpose
Provide structured onboarding checklists for each key project role so that new team members can become productive quickly and understand their responsibilities, tools, and interactions from day one.

---

## Developer Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` to understand all project roles
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-project-management-overview.md` for project approach and principles
- [ ] Set up local development environment per project README
- [ ] Complete first PR following the branching and PR conventions in `docs/octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md`
- [ ] Attend team standup and be introduced to the team
- [ ] Review existing backlog and Definition of Done in the project board
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand current sprint priorities and blockers
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand testing expectations and acceptance criteria process
- [ ] Confirm access to CI/CD pipeline and project board

---

## Product Manager Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` to understand all project roles and interactions
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-project-management-overview.md` and `docs/octoacme-project-planning.md`
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand project status, milestones, and key decisions
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Champion to understand stakeholder groups and current feedback
- [ ] Review the current roadmap and backlog with prioritization rationale
- [ ] Understand success metrics and how they are tracked
- [ ] Confirm access to project board, analytics dashboards, and stakeholder communication channels
- [ ] Attend a sprint planning or review session to observe the team's working rhythm

---

## Project Manager Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` and all docs in `docs/`
- [ ] Review the current Risk Register with the Risk Officer
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand roadmap priorities and upcoming milestones
- [ ] Meet with Release Coordinator to understand the release calendar and upcoming releases
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand current test coverage and quality metrics
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Champion to understand stakeholder expectations and open feedback items
- [ ] Confirm access to project board, communication channels, and documentation repository
- [ ] Take ownership of the weekly status update and ensure distribution list is correct
- [ ] Review the most recent retrospective notes for outstanding action items

---

## Release Coordinator Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` and `docs/octoacme-release-and-deployment.md`
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand the project roadmap and release schedule
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand the QA sign-off process and current test status
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand feature readiness and stakeholder communication needs
- [ ] Review the existing release calendar and identify any upcoming deployments
- [ ] Confirm access to CI/CD pipelines, deployment tooling, and release communication channels
- [ ] Review the Deployment Checklist in `docs/octoacme-release-and-deployment.md` and validate with the team
- [ ] Understand the rollback and incident response process

---

## Risk Officer Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` and `docs/octoacme-risks-and-communication.md`
- [ ] Obtain the current Risk Register and review all open risks and mitigations
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand risk history, escalation paths, and current priorities
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Champion to understand business risk sensitivity and communication preferences
- [ ] Schedule recurring risk review sessions with the team
- [ ] Confirm escalation thresholds and the process for raising issues to the sponsor level
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-risk-escalation-template.md` and ensure the team is familiar with it

---

## QA Lead Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` and `docs/octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md`
- [ ] Review the existing test plan and identify coverage gaps
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand the current state of automated tests and CI configuration
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand sprint cadence, release dates, and quality expectations
- [ ] Meet with Release Coordinator to understand the pre-release sign-off process
- [ ] Review the Definition of Done and validate that QA criteria are included
- [ ] Confirm access to QA tooling, defect tracking, and CI dashboards
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-qa-release-checklist.md` and validate with the team

---

## Stakeholder Champion Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` to understand all roles and interactions
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand stakeholder groups, roadmap, and current priorities
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand project status and upcoming communication milestones
- [ ] Meet with Risk Officer to understand risk posture and how risk impacts stakeholders
- [ ] Attend the first stakeholder briefing and retrospective as an observer
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-stakeholder-communication-templates.md` and the existing communication plan
- [ ] Map stakeholder groups, their communication preferences, and key contacts
- [ ] Confirm access to stakeholder communication channels and reporting artifacts
